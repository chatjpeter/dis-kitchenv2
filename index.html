About Di's Kitchen

At Di’s Kitchen, we bring the taste of Bantayan Island straight to your plate. Known for its crystal-clear waters and fresh seafood, our home is where the best scallops in the Philippines are caught daily.

We began our journey during the pandemic in 2021, starting with selling fresh scallops to friends and neighbors. Over time, we experimented in our kitchen, perfecting recipes that turned our fresh catch into the flavorful baked scallops our customers love today.

Whether you love it plain, spicy, or a perfect mix, every bite carries the freshness of the sea and the warmth of home. Because here at Di’s Kitchen, food isn’t just something you eat—it’s something you remember.
